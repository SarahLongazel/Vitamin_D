The Basics

Vitamins

Vitamins are organic compounds and help absorb minerals into the body.

Vitamin D helps absorb calcium into the body.

VS.

Minerals

Minerals like calcium are inorganic elements.

They are made of single elements you find on the periodic table.

How Vitamin D and the Sun work

When the sun rays hit your skin, your body uses its cholesterol to create vitamin D.
